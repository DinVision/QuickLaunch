Din Quick Launch Toolbar

Overview

Din Quick Launch Toolbar is a customizable auto-hide toolbar for Windows 10/11. It allows you to easily manage your shortcuts, providing a more efficient alternative to the traditional Quick Launch feature.

Features

  • Customizable auto-hide functionality
  • Quick access to your most-used applications
  • Sleek and minimalistic design
  • Windows 10/11 compatible
